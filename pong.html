PONG

January 23rd, 2016 My first foray into game design, PONG is just that: a clone of the original game, down to the bleeps and bloops. The more the ball bounces between paddles, the faster it gets. The game ends when a player gains 10 points. SPACE - Pause Game
Q - Move Up (Left Paddle)
A - Move Down (Left Paddle)
P - Move Up (Right Paddle)
L - Move Down (Right Paddle)
