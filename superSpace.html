SUPER SPACE

October 23rd, 2016 Super Space is a Galaga-esque clone where the you can use different types of ammunition to destroy hordes of enemy ships! But unlike Galaga, you have shields, health, and lives that you have to try to keep as long as possible for when you face the final boss. Destroying ships and collecting currency will increase your total score, and when your score reaches 1000, the boss appears to take you on! The bar at the bottom of the screen is your speed booster, and when the bar is yellow, pressing the 'T' key will make your ship zip around incredibly fast. Once the boss is defeated or if you die at any point, refresh the page to restart the game. Cursor - Aim Homing Missles
W - Move Up
A - Move Left
S - Move Down
D - Move Right
T - Speed Booster
SPACE - Shoot Rockets
Left Click - Shoot Homing Missiles
M - Shoot Multi-Shot
B - Launch Bombs
