VECTOR

January 30th, 2016 Vector was a project made from taking the central mechanics of PONG and twisting them to make a unique game. So instead of the game remaining rigid and rectangular, it was made to be circular and constantly shifting as the game goes on.
Similar to PONG, the ball moves gradually faster as it bounces between paddles. When the ball hits a goal, the goals speed up their rotation and switch direction. When a player's goal has been hit by the ball 5 times, they are eliminated from the game. The last player standing wins. SPACE - Pause Game
Q - Move Clockwise (Player 1)
A - Move Counterclockwise (Player 1)
L - Move Clockwise (Player 2)
P - Move Counterclockwise (Player 2)
B - Move Clockwise (Player 3)
V - Move Counterclockwise (Player 3)
Left Mouse - Move Clockwise (Player 4)
Right Mouse - Move Counterclockwise (Player 4)
